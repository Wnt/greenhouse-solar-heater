About This Project

This system heats a greenhouse using flat-plate solar collectors, a 300L Jäspi tank, and a Wilo circulation pump. Eight motorized valves in a manifold topology allow three operating modes: Solar Charging (collectors → tank), Greenhouse Heating (tank → radiator), and Active Drain (freeze protection). Control runs on Shelly Pro devices with DS18B20 temperature sensors. The system is unpressurized — an open reservoir on top of the tank acts as air separator, expansion buffer, and drainback volume.
