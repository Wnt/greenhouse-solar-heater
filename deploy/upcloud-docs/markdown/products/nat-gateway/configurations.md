# NAT Gateway configurations

NAT and VPN Gateway services are offered in the following configurations:

| Plan | High- availability | Features | VPN tunnels | VPN bandwidth | Throughput | Max connections |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Developer | no | NAT | - | - | 100 Mbit/s | 10,000 |
| Standard | yes | NAT, VPN | 1 | 100 Mbit/s | 500 Mbit/s | 100,000 |
| Production | yes | NAT, VPN | 2 | 300 Mbit/s | 1000 Mbit/s | 100,000 |
| Advanced | yes | NAT, VPN | 10 | 500 Mbit/s | 1000 Mbit/s | 250,000 |
