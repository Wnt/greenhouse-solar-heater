# Audit logs

UpCloud offers an Audit logs feature in the Control Panel to help users maintain comprehensive audit trails within the platform. This feature provides full transparency by offering detailed, tamper-proof records of account and resource activities. Audit logs ensure users and their security teams can meet compliance and regulatory obligations.

UpCloud retains logs for up to 5 years from the creation of each event, and these logs are not deleted even if the associated account is closed. Users can access the most recent 180-day history of their account's activity at no additional cost through the [UpCloud Control Panel](https://hub.upcloud.com/account/audit-logs).

For those requiring extended visibility beyond 180 days, up to the maximum 5-year retention period, please contact our Sales team.
