# Accounts

Registering for an [UpCloud account](https://signup.upcloud.com/) gives users access to our services via the [UpCloud Control Panel](https://hub.upcloud.com/) and [API](https://developers.upcloud.com/).

Each user account is identified by a unique user-selected username and password. By registering for an account through our website, users agree to our [Terms of Service](https://upcloud.com/terms-of-service/) and [Privacy notice](https://upcloud.com/privacy-notice/).
