# Kubernetes available in Amsterdam and Chicago

June 29, 2023
·
[Permalink](/docs/changelog/2023-06-29-uks-in-ams-chi.md)

UpCloud Kubernetes Service is now available in Amsterdam and Chicago, further expanding our reach alongside our existing data centers in Singapore, London, Helsinki, and Frankfurt. This expansion enables users to deploy Kubernetes clusters in key locations worldwide, providing greater flexibility and accessibility for their applications and workloads. Whether you're targeting specific regions or aiming for global coverage, our extended availability ensures that you can leverage UpCloud Kubernetes Service with ease, delivering high-performance, scalable solutions wherever your business needs take you.
