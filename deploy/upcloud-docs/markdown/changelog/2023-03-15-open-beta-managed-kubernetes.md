# Open Beta for UpCloud Managed Kubernetes

March 15, 2023
·
[Permalink](/docs/changelog/2023-03-15-open-beta-managed-kubernetes.md)

We're pleased to announce the Open Beta of UpCloud Managed Kubernetes (UKS). This is a unique opportunity for our users to experience the ease of deploying containerised applications without the burden of managing the infrastructure. The Open Beta allows you to explore the robustness of the control plane management, the scalability of our solutions, and the resilience of built-in self-healing features. Join the Open Beta today and help shape the future of UpCloud Managed Kubernetes!
