# Introducing UpCloud hub dashboard in beta

July 5, 2023
·
[Permalink](/docs/changelog/2023-07-05-hub-dashboard.md)

You can now access the new Dashboard on the Hub. This dashboard offers a comprehensive overview of your account and infrastructure information. With the Dashboard, you can conveniently monitor all your UpCloud resources and gain insights into your infrastructure at a glance. Additionally, you can track daily credit usage and receive the latest news from UpCloud.
