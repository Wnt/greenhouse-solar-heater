## Overview of Developer plans

![](img/dev-deploy-plans.png)

UpCloud offers several Developer plans designed to cater to various needs and budgets. Each plan includes a set amount of resources, such as CPU, memory, and storage, with the option to scale up as your project grows. Key features of these plans include:

- **Affordable pricing**: Competitive rates that suit different budgets.
- **Reliable performance**: High-performance processors paired with our Standard tier SSD storage, up to 10k IOPS, ensure fast and reliable performance.
- **Highly flexible**: Easy to upgrade without downtime using Hot Resizing or downgrade based on your requirements.
- **Advanced features**: All plans include zero-cost egress, free firewall and customisable SDN private networking.

For example, the entry-level plan includes 1 CPU, 1GB of RAM, and 20GB of storage, and transfer is included thanks to our zero-cost egress policy. This plan is ideal for small projects or development environments. Higher-tier plans offer more system memory and storage. resources and additional features like enhanced networking and security options.
